Music Visualization Experiments

Music is one of the most common form of data. Here, we present several experiments exploring innovative ways to visualize music. The track which is used to drive the visualizations is produced by Amir Meludah

p5 and d3

p5js is used for the audio module. d3js is used for the visualization. The amplitude of the audio is embedded to the charge of the circles.

WebAudio, d3 and Canvas

Canvas is used for performent. WebAudio API is used instead of p5 to reduce dependency. d3 is used for visualization.

d3 and AFrame

AFrame is a virtual reality Javascript library. This is showcasing music visualization with virtual reality. With a device that supports virtual reality (Google Cardboard, Oculus Rift, etc. ), one can immerse herself or himself within the music visualization.
